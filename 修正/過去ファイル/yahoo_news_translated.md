# Temperatures to Rise from February 13th: Beware of Avalanches and Falling Snow

**Date:** February 11, 2026 (Wednesday) 15:54
**Source:** tenki.jp
**Article URL:** https://news.yahoo.co.jp/pickup/6569531

The temperature is expected to rise nationwide from February 13th to around the 15th, bringing unusually warm weather for this time of year. In areas with heavy snowfall, the risk of avalanches will increase significantly. Caution is advised regarding falling snow from roofs and deteriorating road conditions. Furthermore, cedar pollen is forecast to become more prevalent in the Kyushu and Kanto regions.
